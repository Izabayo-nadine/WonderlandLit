The Year that Changed Everything:A brilliantly uplifting read for 2024 from the #1 bestseller

 

'Warm, witty and wise' Marian Keyes

Three women, three birthdays, one year that will change everything...

Ginger isn't spending her thirtieth the way she would have planned. Tonight might be the first night of the rest of her life
- or a total disaster. Sam is finally pregnant after years of trying. When her waters break on the morning of her fortieth
birthday, she panics: forget labour, how is she going to be a mother? Callie is celebrating her fiftieth at a big party in her
Dublin home. Then a knock at the door mid-party changes everything... Treat yourself to the heartwarming and
life-affirming new story from international bestseller Cathy Kelly.

About the author

Cathy Kelly is an Irish writer of women's fiction and a former journalist. She has gained international recognition for her
popular novels, which are published globally in many languages. Here are some key points about her: Born in Belfast but
raised in Dublin, Kelly studied at a convent school. She began her writing career with her debut book Woman To Woman,
which became an international bestseller in 1997. Some of her other notable novels include Someone Like You, What She
Wants, Just Between Us, and Best of Friends. Her book Always and Forever was her first hardback to reach number one,
and it topped the overall UK bestseller list in October 2005. Kelly's works have been translated into many different
languages, and she is a number one bestseller in Australia and New Zealand. She has been involved with various charities
and has been an ambassador for UNICEF Ireland since 2005. In 2022, Kelly appeared on the fifth series of the Irish version
of Dancing With the Stars
